Our goal at Viking's Den

The Viking's Den was created to bring a cool, comfortable and relaxing environment for men to get their haircut, beard maintenance or even some styling - the unmatched grooming experience for men.

The mission here is to help men feel relaxed, look good, and feel confident through exceptional hair, shave and grooming services. All Staff is professionally trained to deliver the top of the line service to our clients, though we are a men's salon, we are also proud of the bonds grown over the years through many client/staff interactions. We aim to treat those who walk through our doors not just as clients but also as friends.
